A MESSAGE FROM OSHEEN

Is Music Production Hard ?

Making music is easy! Search for yourself, Martin Garrix was 17 when his famous track "Animals" was released. Avicii was 20 when he made some of his most popular remixes, and Billie Eilish was 18 when "Bad Guy" came out. Even these days there are 12-14 years old producers making professional-sounding, outstanding music, far better than some of those who went to music schools and even have a professional degree in making music.

What I learnt in last 5 years

How do you produce melodies, hooks, and ideas?
You don't need to know all the music theory in the world! You should know how to recreate any melody you hear or create any melody/idea that comes to your mind.

How do you convert your ideas to Tracks?
There are only limited ways to create "professional" tracks, listen to some of Michael Jackson's songs, those songs were released in the 80s and still sound professional, even by today's standards. How come they were able to produce it 30 years ago? You don't need to own every other plugin that comes out. The basics of music production are still the same.

How do you make your track sound more professional?
Sometimes, your track still lacks something, and you can't figure out what! even though it sounds great, it doesn't quite sound right compared to other professional tracks. What are you lacking?

Why this Workshop tho?

Trust me, you do not have to take expensive, overwhelming music courses and waste your money. Neither you have to enrol for Music Schools that will charge you for their "infrastructure and studio equipments". This workshop is more than enough to kickstart your music producer journey.
