OSHEEN's CONTENT USAGE POLICY

PROJECT FLP's

FLP projects by OSHEEN are "zip downloads" that are provided along with tutorial videos. These are work of OSHEEN and are mostly given for educational purposes only. Projects can be used in your own track under following conditions :

1. Credits must be given in title/description stating that the original creator is indeed OSHEEN, by providing social media links that should include Youtube, Facebook and Soundcloud.

2. You are allowed to create "remix" of a project. However this prohibits you from uploading/releasing the project under your own name without taking permission from original creator. Also you will have to change the structure and melody of the project

3. Uploading any tutorial video based on original FLP or videos showcasing original FLP needs prior approval from OSHEEN, failing to which may cause a COPYRIGHT INFRINGEMENT against channel/individual.

4. No part of OSHEEN's logo/branding should be reproduced anywhere in your video/thumbnail/album art/banner without apt permission.

5. You are not allowed to redistribute original FLP, you must NOT include any download links.

MUSIC

All tracks can be used on social media in monetized/non monetized videos under the circumstance that Osheen get credited. Using music on social media without crediting is not tolerated and will unless the matter has been discussed and approved prior, result in said video being reported for copyright infringement. Please adhere to following guidelines:

1. Credits must be given in title/description stating that the original creator is indeed OSHEEN, by providing social media links that should include Youtube, Facebook and Soundcloud.

2. You are allowed to create "remix" of a song and you can upload/release the project under your own name after giving credits to original creator.

If you wish to use Osheen's music without giving credit, you can do so by purchasing a license. In that case reach out to via emailm describe your project through the contact form below and I will get back to you with details.

4. There are two scenarios when Osheen's music is not free to use : If you want to use my music on social media without the need to credit me and/or If you wish to use my music in films, games, TV programs/commercials
